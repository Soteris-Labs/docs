---
title: "Artifact records"
tag: "Public"
---

An artifact record is something Soteris produces during an engagement: a draft, a finding set, a classification, or a deliverable candidate. Artifacts move through their own status, separate from the engagement lifecycle, so you can tell a work-in-progress draft from a released deliverable.

## Status values

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| draft | Being prepared; not for external reliance. |
| review\_ready | Complete enough to enter review. |
| released | Authorized and made available within access. |
| superseded | Replaced by a newer version. |

## Shape

An artifact record identifies its engagement, its kind, its status, and its version. A minimal, synthetic example:

```json
{
  "schemaVersion": "0",
  "artifactId": "art_7b31",
  "engagementId": "eng_9f2c",
  "kind": "finding_set",
  "status": "review_ready",
  "version": 2,
  "updatedAt": "2026-01-16T09:20:00Z"
}
```

Only a `released` artifact is externally reliable, and only through its released record and receipt. A `draft` or `review_ready` artifact is internal working state. See [Release records](/packets/release-packet) and [Verification receipts](/packets/verification-receipt).
