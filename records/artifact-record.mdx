An artifact record describes something Soteris produces during an engagement: a draft, a finding set, a classification, or a deliverable candidate. Artifacts carry their own status, separate from the engagement lifecycle, so a work-in-progress draft is never confused with a released deliverable.

## Status values

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `draft` | Being prepared. Not for external reliance. |
| `review_ready` | Complete enough to enter review. |
| `released` | Authorized and made available within access. |
| `superseded` | Replaced by a newer version. |

## Shape

An artifact record identifies its engagement, its kind, its status, and its version. A minimal, synthetic example:

```json title="artifact-record.json"
{
  "schemaVersion": "0",
  "artifactId": "art_7b31",
  "engagementId": "eng_9f2c",
  "kind": "finding_set",
  "status": "review_ready",
  "version": 2,
  "updatedAt": "2026-01-16T09:20:00Z"
}
```

## Reliance

<Check>
Only a `released` artifact is externally reliable, and only through its [release record](/records/release-record) and [receipt](/records/verification-receipt). A `draft` or `review_ready` artifact is internal working state: it exists, but it is not official.
</Check>
